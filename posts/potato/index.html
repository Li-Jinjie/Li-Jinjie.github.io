Development of a 3D Simulator for Large-Scale Heterogeneous Swarm Robots

2022-11-01
1 min read

Overview

This project is required to develope a simulator that supports (1) over 1000 robot nodes, (2) 6-DoF dynamic models of four model types, including fixed-wing UAVs, quadcopters, tilt-rotor UAVs, and vehicles, (3) range of vision and collision, (4) 3D visualization, and (5) a dataset to build for off-line model training and an interface with Gym for on-line DRL training.
